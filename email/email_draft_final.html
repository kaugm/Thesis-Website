Confidentiality and Data Privacy  

Participation in this survey is voluntary. All answers provided will be kept anonymous and confidential. By clicking the button to take the survey, you hereby give electronic consent and agree to participate in the survey.   

      • Participation
      • Your participation in this survey is voluntary. You may refuse to take part in this research if you choose so. Answering all questions is required for successful completion of the survey, however respondents may withdraw from the survey at any time without penalty.
      • Benefits
      • Respondents who successfully complete the survey may voluntarily enter their email addresses at the end of the survey to be entered into a lottery to win a gift card valued at $50 USD (or in equivalent currency for Europe and China). We will reach out to the winning respondent to compensate them as soon as data collection is finished.
      • Risks
      • There is the risk that you may find some of the demographic questions to be sensitive in nature. Some of the demographic questions pertain to gender/sex, individual income level, and age.
      • Confidentiality & Data Privacy
      • Your survey answers are recorded and securely stored on Qualtrics. The survey will NOT collect any personal identifying information such as name, identification numbers, date of birth, addresses, phone numbers, or IP addresses. The only identifying information collected is email addresses, which the respondent may enter at his or her discretion.
      • Before data analysis, all email data entries will be separated from all other data and be used solely for the purpose of conducting the lottery and compensating the winning respondent. Additionally, responses will be randomized to further anonymization. All data downloaded from Qualtrics will be stored in password-protected format. The data file(s) will never be uploaded to the internet, nor stored on any cloud platform such as Google Drive or Dropbox. All analysis will be conducted offline. After the research is complete, all data will be deleted permanently.
      • The only persons able to view the raw data will be us, the four master's students conducting the research as well as our academic advisor.
