Upon clicking the link, you will be randomly placed into a survey group on Qualtrics and prompted to enter the survey code. This survey code can be found in the email sent to valid respondents.

SURVEY CONFIDENTIALITY & DATA PRIVACY

  • Participation
  • Your participation in this survey is voluntary. You may refuse to take part in this research if you choose so. Respondents may withdraw at any time without penalty, however answering all questions is required for successful completion of the survey and to be considered for the chance to win compensation.
  • Benefits
  • Respondents who successfully complete the survey may voluntarily enter their email addresses at the end of the survey to be entered into a lottery to win a gift card valued at $50 USD (or in equivalent currency for Europe and China). We will reach out to the winning respondent to compensate them as soon as data collection is finished.
  • Risks
  • There are no the risks involved with this survey. Some of the demographic questions pertain to age, gender, individual income level, and personal investments amount. These types of questions typical for demographic questions and are routinely asked in surveys.
  • Confidentiality & Data Privacy
  • Your survey answers are recorded and securely stored on Qualtrics. The survey will NOT collect any personal identifying information such as name, identification numbers, date of birth, addresses, phone numbers, or IP addresses. The only identifying information collected is email addresses, which the respondent may enter at his or her discretion.
  • Before data analysis, all email data entries will be separated from all other data and be used solely for the purpose of conducting the lottery and compensating the winning respondent. Once the winning respondent is selected and successfully compensated, email data will be deleted leaving purely anonymous data. Additionally, responses will be randomized to further anonymization.
  • All data downloaded from Qualtrics will be stored in password-protected format. The data file(s) will never be uploaded to the internet, nor stored on any cloud platform such as Google Drive or Dropbox. All analysis will be conducted offline.
  • The only persons able to view the raw data will be us, the four master's students conducting the research, as well as our academic advisor.
  • ELECTRONIC CONSENT
  • By clicking the button to take the survey, you hereby give electronic consent and:
    • (1) You have read the above information
    • (2) You voluntarily agree to participate in the study
    • (3) You are above 18 years of age

Please ensure javascript is enabled in your browser (enabled by default). Javascript is required to run the sorting function when the button is clicked. Verified working in Chrome v73.0, Firefox v65.0.2, and Mobile Safari v12.
